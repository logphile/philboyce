The Shape of Staying

Literary Fiction · 59,000 words

A man mistakes management for preparation.

Set on a quiet street in Virginia, THE SHAPE OF STAYING follows a man who begins assembling fragments of a letter he hopes might explain everything that could not be repaired.

“He had become, over the years, an expert in the maintenance of things that were already broken.”

Phil Boyce lives in Virginia and writes literary fiction.
